04 — Voice & Tone
Brand Voice
Authoritative, not intimidating
Communicate expertise and credibility with plain language. Clients are navigating complex legal processes — reassure them with clarity, not jargon.
Reliable, not robotic
Write with warmth. Document authentication is often stressful — the brand should feel like a trusted partner, not a government form.
Precise, not bureaucratic
Every word should earn its place. Avoid filler phrases and corporate speak. Be specific about timelines, steps, and outcomes.
✓ Do say
"Your FBI background check apostille will be ready in 3–5 business days."
✗ Don't say
"We leverage synergistic solutions to facilitate expedient documentation processing."
✓ Do say
"Not sure which service you need? We'll help you figure it out."
✗ Don't say
"Pursuant to international authentication requirements, documentation compliance is mandatory."
